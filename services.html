Barrier Sprays

The best way to keep your yard pest-free is with recurring barrier spray treatments. In less than an hour, your home and family will be protected from pests and disease. We will identify the primary breeding grounds and treat those areas to prevent and eliminate pest populations.

MosquitoBanUSA uses only pyrethrum-based spray solutions because we want the best for our customers. They are naturally derived from the chrysanthemum flower and are very effective against pests without harming anything else. In fact, these same solutions are used in lice shampoos intended for direct skin contact.

For a naturally derived, low-cost solution against mosquitoes and other pests, barrier sprays are the most effective choice.
