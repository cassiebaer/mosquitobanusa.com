Automatic Misting Systems

Sometimes Bill doesn't feel like hurting mosquitoes. He can be a very sensitive man. That's why he convinced some smart scientists to build the automagic misting system. Kind of like your grandfather, but a lot less mean, Bill's Patented Automagic Misting System spreads unsolicited advice at inopportune times, driving sensitive vampires away.

Barrier Sprays

Bill doesn't believe in borders. They keep the right people out and the wrong people in. However, Bill does enjoy building up barriers against vampires so that he can sit and /sarcasm in peace. For a modest fee, Bill will come and sprinkle some less-than-encouraging words around your yard, so that you also can have peace.
